⬣ KF WorkOrder Knowledge Graph — Manufacturing Maintenance Use Case
Work orders — preventive, corrective, and inspection maintenance jobs — are captured from two source systems (SAP-PM via OData v4 and MES-PROD via REST/JSON), curated and modelled into OWL2 ontology TTL files that formally define the structure and relationships of every entity in the manufacturing domain. These TTL files are loaded into Neo4j via n10s, deduplicated by a five-rule Entity Resolution engine, and connected in a knowledge graph to equipment assets, production lines, technicians, and compliance policies. The result is a single queryable source of truth accessed by three specialised agents: the Maintenance Planner, the Compliance Monitor, and the Root Cause Analyst.
